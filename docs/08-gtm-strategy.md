## The Sales Motion

### Step 1: LAND with Compliance

**Why compliance first:**
- Every bank/NBFC MUST do it (regulatory mandate)
- No revenue risk (not touching customer-facing flows)
- Immediate ROI (measurable time savings)
- Creates internal champion (Chief Compliance Officer)
- LOW procurement barrier (can start as a POC)

**The pitch:**
> "What if every RBI circular was analyzed, with action items extracted and deadlines tracked, within minutes of release instead of days?"

**Demo flow:**
1. Take their most recent complex RBI circular
2. Run through Claude with the RBI Circular Analyzer prompt
3. Show structured output: summary, action items, deadlines, impact assessment
4. Compare: "Your team took 3 days. Claude did this in 90 seconds."
5. Multiply: "You get 500 circulars a year. That's 1,500 analyst-days saved."

### Step 2: EXPAND to Operations

**After compliance is won (month 3-4):**
- Credit underwriting co-pilot (for NBFCs)
- KYC document review automation
- Customer grievance response drafting
- Collections strategy intelligence

**The expand pitch:**
> "You saw what Claude did for compliance. Now imagine the same for your underwriting team, which processes 200 loan files a day."

### Step 3: TRANSFORM to Core

**After operations wins (month 6+):**
- Treasury research and ALCO support
- Personalized customer advisory (wealth/insurance)
- Risk model documentation and validation
- Board MIS and reporting automation

## Objection Handling

### "We already use GPT-4 / Azure OpenAI"
> "GPT-4 works great for general tasks. But in regulated finance, you need an AI that explains its reasoning, doesn't hallucinate on compliance matters, and was built safety-first. Ask your compliance team how comfortable they are using GPT-4 outputs for RBI reporting. Claude was purpose-built for exactly this."

### "Data can't leave India / our premises"
> "Claude is available through AWS, which has data centers in Mumbai (ap-south-1). Your data never leaves India. For the most sensitive workloads, we can discuss VPC deployments and dedicated instances."

### "We need to build internally"
> "Building a foundation model that meets RBI's FREE-AI framework requirements would cost you crores and years. With Claude, you get a model that's already SOC 2 certified, bias-tested, and deployed at Goldman Sachs — and you can be live in weeks, not years."

### "Our board is concerned about AI safety"
> "That's exactly why Claude exists. Anthropic was founded specifically to build safe AI. Our Constitutional AI approach is designed for exactly this concern. We'd be happy to present to your board on our safety methodology — it's actually a selling point, not a risk."

### "Pricing is too high for Indian market"
> "Let's calculate the ROI. If Claude saves your 10-person compliance team 40 hours/week, that's ~INR 2 Cr/year in analyst time alone. The API cost for that volume is a fraction. The ROI payback is typically under 3 months."

### "We need to wait for DPDPA rules to be finalized"
> "Claude's enterprise offering is already designed for global data protection standards (GDPR, which is stricter than DPDPA). By adopting Claude now, you'll already be ahead of whatever DPDPA mandates. We can help you build compliant-by-design from day one."

## Competitive Positioning

### vs. Azure OpenAI (GPT-4)
- **Their strength**: Already embedded in Microsoft ecosystem, Azure relationships
- **Our edge**: Safety-first design, longer context, lower hallucination in finance, RBI FREE-AI alignment
- **Key battleground**: Banks already on Azure will default to OpenAI. Need to win on safety narrative.

### vs. Google Gemini (Vertex AI)
- **Their strength**: GCP enterprise relationships, BigQuery integration
- **Our edge**: Focused financial services expertise, Constitutional AI
- **Key battleground**: Data-heavy analytics use cases. Gemini has context window but reliability is lower.

### vs. Build-Your-Own (Open Source)
- **Their strength**: Full control, no vendor lock-in, data stays on-prem
- **Our edge**: Time-to-value, compliance certifications, enterprise support
- **Key battleground**: Large banks with in-house AI teams. Need to show Claude adds value on top.
